**Ingredients**
1 part mayonnaise
1 part sour cream/yogurt ranch dip
chipotle peppers in adobo sauce

**Method**
Finely chop chipotle peppers.
Combine all ingredients.
Chill for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight.

**Settings/Pairing**
Black Bean Burgers
Santa Fe Turkey Wraps
